F.A.Q.


  • What is a Ward?
    A ward is something you can buy that when placed grants vision in a given area on the map
  • What is the difference between the trinket ward and the vision ward?
    The trinket ward grants vision for a given amount of time and cannont be seen by the enemy. The vision ward is permanent but can be see and destroyed by the enemy.
